A Resort Rooted in
Ancient Letters

Each cottage at Beit Smar carries the name of a Phoenician letter — the very alphabet born on this Levantine coast, the ancestor of all Western writing. From Alep to Zayin, eleven dwellings, eleven characters, one story.

Set among the vineyards of Batroun, minutes from the Old Souk and the Smar Jbeil Citadel, this boutique resort offers a rare convergence of heritage, nature, and refined hospitality.
