## 1. SmarHero — Billboard Forest Hero

**File:** `frontend/src/pages/smar/showcase/SmarHero.jsx` (created)

- Background: `journey_forest.jpg` (forest entrance with stone gate)
- Logo video (`hero_video.mp4`) overlaid precisely on the white billboard panel in the image
- Aspect-ratio wrapper (`min(100vw, calc(100vh * 1.792))`) keeps billboard % positions stable across all viewport sizes
- Safe scroll parallax: passive `scroll` listener mutates DOM refs directly — NO `useScroll` / `useTransform` (banned: FM12 + React 19 StrictMode crash)
- Outer container `200vh` + `sticky` inner `100vh` so parallax has room to animate
- Billboard constants tuned iteratively via browser screenshots:
  ```js
  { left: '79.5%', top: '45%', width: '13%', height: '17%',

  ```
